Gosfield Lake is a 36-acre lake in a private family-run leisure park in rural Essex. The lake and surrounding countryside are a natural beauty spot. The lake's natural sheltering provides ideal skiing conditions.

We run a Ski Nautique 200 alongside our Ski Nautique Carbon, we've been running Ski Nautiques for 23 years, these are the most commonly used tournament boats in international competitions, allowing our tournament skiers to practise.
