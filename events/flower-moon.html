About the Swim

Join us for a magical evening open water swim as we celebrate the beauty of the Flower Moon. Glide through calm waters beneath the rising moon, surrounded by nature and a peaceful atmosphere.

This is the perfect way to welcome the warmer months — an unforgettable experience on the beautiful 36-acre Gosfield Lake.

The Venue

Gosfield Lake is a 36-acre lake in a private family-run leisure park in rural Essex. The sheltered lake provides calm, consistent conditions ideal for building confidence and improving technique.

Directions: Sat Nav with postcode CO9 1UD, once close follow the brown leisure signs for Gosfield Lake.
